LCS Social Media Setup

Configure your social media automation in 5 steps

Telegram Bot

Step 1 of 5
  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather
  2. Send /newbot and follow the prompts to name your bot
  3. Copy the bot token and paste it below
  1. Search for @userinfobot on Telegram and message it
  2. It will reply with your numeric chat ID — paste it below
Numeric ID only — this restricts the bot to respond only to you

Gemini AI

Step 2 of 5
  1. Click here to open Google AI Studio
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "Create API Key" and copy it

Completely free — no credit card needed. 15 requests/min.

Image Hosting

Step 3 of 5
  1. Click here to open imgbb
  2. Create a free account (or sign in)
  3. Copy your API key from the dashboard

Free image hosting — used temporarily so Instagram and Facebook can access your images.

LinkedIn

Step 4 of 5
  1. Click here to open LinkedIn Developer Portal
  2. Click "Create App" — fill in your company name, app name, and logo
  3. Go to the "Auth" tab and copy your Client ID and Client Secret
  4. In the "Auth" tab, add this redirect URL:
    http://localhost:3456/callback/linkedin
  5. Go to "Products" tab and request access to "Community Management API"

Facebook & Instagram

Step 5 of 5
  1. Click here to open Meta Developer Portal
  2. Click "Create App" → choose "Business" type
  3. In your app settings, add the product "Instagram Graph API"
  4. Go to "App Settings" → "Basic" and copy your App ID and App Secret
  5. Under "Facebook Login" → "Settings", add this redirect URL:
    http://localhost:3456/callback/meta
✓

Setup Complete!

Your credentials have been saved to .env

Next — install as a background service:

npm run install-service

Registers the bot with macOS launchd so it starts on login and auto-restarts on crash.

Or start manually for testing:

npm start

Then open Telegram and send /generate to your bot.
