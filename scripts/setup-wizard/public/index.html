LCS Social Media Setup

Configure your social media automation

Welcome

First time? Setup takes about 40 minutes — you'll connect Telegram, Gemini AI, image hosting, LinkedIn, and Facebook/Instagram.

Already set up on another machine? Restore your config in seconds.

API Keys

Step 1 of 3
Telegram Bot
  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather
  2. Send /newbot and follow the prompts to name your bot
  3. Copy the bot token and paste it below
  4. Search for @userinfobot on Telegram and message it
  5. It will reply with your numeric chat ID — paste it below
Numeric ID only — restricts the bot to respond only to you
Gemini AI
  1. Click here to open Google AI Studio
  2. Sign in with your Google account and click "Create API Key"

Free — no credit card needed.

Image Hosting (imgbb)
  1. Click here to open imgbb
  2. Create a free account (or sign in) and copy your API key

Free — used so Instagram can access your images via public URL.

LinkedIn

Step 2 of 3
  1. Click here to open LinkedIn Developer Portal
  2. Click "Create App" — fill in your company name, app name, and logo
  3. Go to the "Auth" tab and copy your Client ID and Client Secret
  4. In the "Auth" tab, add this redirect URL:
    http://localhost:3456/callback/linkedin
  5. Go to "Products" tab and request access to "Community Management API"

Facebook & Instagram

Step 3 of 3
  1. Click here to open Meta Developer Portal
  2. Click "Create App" → choose "Business" type
  3. In your app settings, add the product "Instagram Graph API"
  4. Go to "App Settings" → "Basic" and copy your App ID and App Secret
  5. Under "Facebook Login" → "Settings", add this redirect URL:
    http://localhost:3456/callback/meta
